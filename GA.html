0. Problem description



Let's say we need to make schedule for room/time with courses. How are we going to tune? It could be easy enough to do by manual way when it is not too much to tune, however, If it is too big to schedule it manually, then it would not be the best way to do it. Those are courses we have for comming semester, and we want to match room and time for each courses without any problems.

Why can it not be the right answer?

Prof.A :
2 lectures at the same time.

Third year students :
2 classes at the same time.

Room 1 :
at most 20 people.

Constraints
1. Each Prof, at most 1 class at one period
2. Each Students group, at most 1 class at one period
3. Room capacity >= students of course.
4. Room cannot be double booked.

Evaluation function : 1/(1+number of constraints)
If evaluation function is equal to 1, then it can be possible solution.

1. The concept of Genetic Algorithm


Genetic algorithm is the way of finding the answer imitating the genetic system. All people get their genetic information from their parents, half from mom and half from dad. and sometimes, mutation occurs which can change the genetic information. In the Genetic Algorithm , each individual contains the information of answer. It does not have to be right. Each individual is now evaluated how much it is close to the correct answer. Depends on the fitness value of individual, it varies the chance of being parents. Then, How can we represent this information of courses and the classes for each individual? We can use array.
